METHODOLOGY Sources, media, and editorial limits

Transparency on what this static site can and cannot claim. It is a curated index, not a substitute for physical archives or label contracts.

1. Scope

ARCHIV — Albin Julius summarises publicly indexed facts about the musician and producer Albin Julius (1967–2022). Assertions on the timeline and motif cards are tied to rows in SOURCES_ARCHIV.md; open questions live in QUESTIONS_ARCHIV.md.

2. Databases (MusicBrainz, Wikidata, Metal Archives, SR-Archiv)

Identifiers are copied for navigation. Schema mismatches between databases are normal; we do not reconcile them silently. When two sources disagree, the HTML should say so or omit the detail until documented.

3. Images & hero

The performance photograph is File:Albin-rejoice-e1653633850190.jpg on Wikimedia Commons (author SneQ, date 10 June 2021, CC BY-SA 4.0). The local path is images/albin/albin-julius-performance-2021-sneq-cc-by-sa-4-0.jpg. Neutral SVG motifs (favicon, grid) are site-only assets.

4. Video & audio hosts

Outbound players (YouTube, Internet Archive, etc.) remain under the host’s terms and metadata chain. Treat embedded or linked files as mediated documents, not as certified archival masters.

5. Political material

Where the legacy template carried political iconography, this rebuild removes it from layout and branding. Any future discussion of political context must follow the same source-forward rule: cite a specific print or web row before asserting a fact.

6. Domain placeholder

URLs currently use https://archiv-albinjulius.example (reserved TLD). Replace with the production hostname before publication — see editorial notes.
