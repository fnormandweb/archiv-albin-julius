## 2. Short biographical summary (draft ≤ ~90 words)

Albin Julius Martinek (16 October 1967 – 4 May 2022) was an Austrian musician associated with **industrial** and **martial music**. He is widely documented as the driving figure behind **Der Blutharsch** (1996–2022, Austria; indexed with Vienna as the group’s documented locality in MusicBrainz). Earlier activity includes **The Moon Lay Hidden Beneath a Cloud** (1992–1999). Institutional indexing appears in the **SR-Archiv** (Austrian popular music archives). Primary public output is tied to his label **WKN** (Austria), with additional label activity reported as **HauRuck!** (to be expanded only when independently sourced).

*Place of birth:* state **Austria** only until `QUESTIONS_ARCHIV.md` §1 is resolved.
